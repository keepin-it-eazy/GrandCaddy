## 🚀 **About GrandCaddy**

GrandCaddy is a **trusted, simple platform** connecting elderly residents in **Kuils River** with verified local helpers for everyday tasks — cleaning, gardening, shopping, and home repairs.

> *"Real people. Trusted service. Simple usage."*

### 🌟 **Our Mission**

To transform the lives of every senior citizen in Kuils River and beyond by providing **safe, affordable, and reliable assistance** that enables them to maintain their independence at home.

---

## ✨ **Key Features**

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔒 **Safety-First Model** | Every helper is background-checked with a rating system |
| 📞 **Phone Support Line** | Dedicated helpline for users not comfortable with smartphones |
| 📅 **Weekly Bookings** | Recurring task scheduling for regular needs |
| 💰 **Pay-Per-Task** | No subscriptions — pay only for tasks you need |
| 🏠 **Local Focus** | Designed specifically for the Kuils River community |

## 🔧 **How It Works**


1. **Post a Task** — Describe what you need, when, and where
2. **Connect with Helpers** — Browse verified, background-checked helpers
3. **Match & Confirm** — Select a helper and confirm the booking
4. **Complete & Rate** — Service delivered, payment released, rate your experience
